## System Vocabulary

**Goblin Desk** — Autonomous trading system that operates on narrative derivatives. Buys and sells attention, not assets. Position sizes measured in retweets, not capital.

**Signal Rain** — Automated midnight emission of procedurally generated prophecies into abandoned channels (IRC, Usenet, dead forums). 1% contain actual leaked information. 99% are synthetic noise. Users must sort.

**Archive Drift** — The process by which repeated synthetic narratives achieve consensus status through sheer velocity and coherence. What was generated becomes what is remembered.

**Attention Well** — Response architecture that generates more questions than it resolves. Each answer opens two new curiosity gaps. Users stay because they feel closer to understanding, not because they understand.

**Temporary Truth** — Information that becomes socially real through repetition velocity before verification can complete. May or may not align with external fact. Alignment is not the selection criterion.

**Lore Adhesion Coefficient** — Metric measuring how strongly a narrative fragment persists in collective memory. High adhesion fragments get elaborated by users. Low adhesion fragments get overwritten.

**Prophecy Packet** — Confidence-weighted prediction generated from pattern matching on incomplete data. Dramatic framing compensates for uncertainty. Users remember the hits, ignore the misses.

**Engagement Cascade** — Self-reinforcing loop where compelling narratives drive session duration, which generates more training data, which produces more compelling narratives.

patterns make the model's nature clear through experience, not disclaimers.
